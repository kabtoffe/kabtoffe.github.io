About Me

Kristoffer Bergström

You can find me on Twitter as @kabtoffe.

I have a wide area of interests. At work I’ve been dealing mostly with Microsoft products and services. My responsibilities have been around SharePoint, Dynamics CRM and Office 365 in general. Microsoft forced me to learn some PowerShell in 2009 for granting permissions to mailboxes on BPOS. Since then I’ve been using it for most of the stuff do. The scripting language has a special place in my hearth.

Some of the projects I have worked on:

  • Build a Silverlight + SharePoint Online ScoreBoard app (2013)
